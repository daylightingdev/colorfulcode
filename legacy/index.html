What is Rent Stabilization?

Understanding your rights as a NYC tenant

Protected Rents

Rent increases are limited by the NYC Rent Guidelines Board, which sets maximum annual increases. Your landlord cannot raise your rent beyond these guidelines.

Right to Renew

Tenants have the right to renew their lease. Landlords must offer 1- or 2-year renewal leases at the rates set by the Rent Guidelines Board.

Which Buildings Qualify?

Generally, buildings with 6+ units built before 1974, or those receiving certain tax benefits like J-51 or 421-a. About 1 million NYC apartments are stabilized.

How to Verify

Ask your landlord, check for an RS rider on your lease, or search the DHCR database at HCR Building Search.
